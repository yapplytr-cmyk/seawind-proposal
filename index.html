04 — What the website does

Four surfaces. One identity.

Model Explorer

Cinematic page per model. 3D walk-through, sail plan, deck layout, launch timeline, nearest dealer with chat. Compares across the range in one click.

Configurator & reservation

Hull, layout, propulsion (including hybrid-electric), helm electronics, commissioning port — live price, savable, sharable with a dealer, followed by a reservation deposit.

Dealer network

Every dealer on a world map. Chat, send a spec, book a sea trial at Bodrum, Palma, Annapolis, Sydney. Dealer portal behind the scenes.

Stories & Journal

Owner voyages, factory updates, technical deep-dives. The blog becomes a story engine with hero films and typography that behaves like a magazine.
